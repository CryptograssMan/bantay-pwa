Frequently Asked Questions

What is SAFE ka ba?

SAFE ka ba? is a public service web application designed to provide calamity alerts, starting with earthquakes. Official data is updated every 15 minutes to ensure you have timely information. The app's primary goal is to help you stay informed and connected with those you care about during critical events. Future updates will include alerts for other types of calamities.

How does the safety status work?

When you mark yourself as "Safe", your status is set for a default period of 4 hours. Other members in your groups will see a countdown timer next to your name. If you do not update your status after four hours, it will automatically revert to "Unknown." You can mark yourself as "Safe" again at any time to restart the timer, or change your status to "Needs Help."

How do I create and manage groups?

You can create groups to monitor the safety status of anyone you care about—family, friends, your community, or employees. When creating a group, please use only letters and numbers for the name; avoid special characters to ensure the invite code works correctly. You can then share the unique invite link to add members. The safety check feature works anywhere in the world, and there is no limit to the number of groups you can create.

How is this different from other apps?

Many calamity apps on the market are commercial products. They often require payment for a "pro" version to unlock full functionality or to remove intrusive advertisements. SAFE ka ba? is different. It is a public service initiative, not a business. This means it will always be free and will never have ads. Our focus is on public safety, not profit.

Is it really free? Do I need to register?

Yes, this service is forever free in service of the Filipino people. We believe safety information is a right, not a product. The app is also completely anonymous. You do not need to register, provide an email address, or give your phone number. No login is ever required.

Why should I "Add to Home Screen"?

When you add this app to your phone's home screen, it acts like a regular app, giving it a stable and protected storage space. This is the most important step to ensure your anonymous session and group memberships are saved permanently. If you only use it in a browser tab, your phone's operating system may automatically clear its data, causing you to lose your groups.

How can I help improve the app?

Your feedback is vital! You can help by using the Feedback button to send us suggestions or report any bugs you find. You can also use the rating buttons in the footer to let us know if you like the app.
