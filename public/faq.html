Frequently Asked Questions

What is SAFE ka ba?

SAFE ka ba? is a public service web application designed to provide calamity alerts, starting with earthquakes. Official data is updated every 15 minutes to ensure you have timely information. The app's primary goal is to help you stay informed and connected with those you care about during critical events. Future updates will include alerts for other types of calamities.

How does the safety status work?

When you mark yourself as "Safe", your status is set for a default period of 4 hours. Other members in your groups will see a countdown timer next to your name. If you do not update your status after four hours, it will automatically revert to "Unknown." You can mark yourself as "Safe" again at any time to restart the timer, or change your status to "Needs Help."

How do I change my name?

When you are viewing the members of a group, you can change your own name by double-tapping on it. A prompt will appear allowing you to enter a new name that will be visible to everyone in your groups.

How do I create and manage groups?

You can create groups to monitor the safety status of anyone you care about—family, friends, your community, or employees. When creating a group, please use only letters and numbers for the name; avoid special characters to ensure the invite code works correctly. You can then share the unique invite link to add members. The safety check feature works anywhere in the world, and there is no limit to the number of groups you can create.

How do I fix location errors on iPhone?

If you get a "location access denied" error on your iPhone even though you've enabled it, you may need to check your main iOS settings for Safari. Here’s how to fix it:

  1. Go to your iPhone's Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services and ensure it's turned on.
  2. On that same screen, scroll down to find Safari Websites in the list and tap it.
  3. Make sure that "While Using the App" is checked and that "Precise Location" is turned on.
  4. Finally, reload the app. The location banner should now work correctly.

Last Resort: If Safari continues to block location access after checking all settings, we recommend trying the Google Chrome browser on your iPhone, which often handles location permissions more consistently for web apps.

How do I add this to my Home Screen?

Adding this app to your home screen is the most important step to ensure your anonymous session and group memberships are saved permanently. If you only use it in a browser tab, your phone may clear its data, causing you to lose your groups.

For iPhone & iPad (using Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down the list and tap on Add to Home Screen.
  3. Confirm by tapping Add on the next screen.

For Android (using Chrome)

  1. Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap on Install app (or Add to Home Screen).
  3. Follow the prompts to confirm the installation.
